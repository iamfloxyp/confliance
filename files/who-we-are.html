Our Story

Simplifying insurance for Nigeria, one client at a time.

Confiance Insurance Brokers Limited is a professional insurance brokerage and employee benefits advisory firm committed to delivering client-focused services to individuals and corporate bodies across Nigeria.

We understand that every client has unique needs and risk exposures. Our experienced team works diligently to match clients with the right coverage from our extensive network of leading insurance underwriters.

Our name — Confiance (French for trust) — reflects the foundation of everything we do. We earn and keep trust by being transparent, professional, and genuinely committed to your best interest.
