Our Products

Comprehensive employee benefits solutions

We support our clients to design and review employee benefit schemes that meet the aspirations of all stakeholders — from the boardroom to the shop floor.

Scheme Designs and Reviews

Our services include benefit design and review, actuarial valuation, cost determination, and regulatory compliance — ensuring your schemes remain relevant and sustainable.

Actuarial Services

Annual actuarial valuation of schemes; assessing liability obligations of gratuity schemes; advising on scheme funding options; and valuation assumptions setting.

Pension Industry Support Services

Business Process Review and Automation for Operators; Electronic Document Management System (EDMS); Review of Standards of Operations; Trainings and capacity building.

Our actuarial services are delivered in conjunction with Becoda Consulting, bringing specialist actuarial expertise to complement our advisory practice.
